About Me

I am a learning designer and instructional design professional with experience supporting course design, faculty development, LMS transitions, accessibility, and digital learning strategy. My work spans online and in-person course development, faculty consultations, workshops, multimedia production, AI integration, and student support.

I currently serve as a Learning Designer at Quinnipiac University and also teach as an Adjunct Instructor. My background includes instructional design and e-learning support roles at both Quinnipiac University and Sacred Heart University.

My interests sit at the intersection of teaching, learning, technology, accessibility, and emerging AI tools. I enjoy building practical learning experiences that are thoughtful, usable, and engaging.
